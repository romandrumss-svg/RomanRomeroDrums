About Me

Roman Romero is a professional drummer from Huntington Beach, CA. He has been playing drums since the age of 2, performing on albums and stages across the U.S. In 2020, he recorded on an album with Sublime with Rome. He has also drummed with Long Beach Dub Allstars and Ras-1, adding to his versatile experience in reggae, ska, punk, and rock.

In June 2025, Roman welcomed his first daughter into the world. As a family-oriented musician, he is driven not only by passion for his craft, but also by building a strong future for his family.

Roman treats drumming as both an art and a business — offering lessons, live performances, studio recording, and touring opportunities for artists and bands who want a professional, reliable drummer.
