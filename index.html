About Me

I’ve been playing drums since I was 2 years old, on and off. In 2020, I recorded an album with Sublime with Rome, gigged, and played all around the United States. I’ve performed on multiple albums, worked with some of the best in the industry, and continue to bring high-energy performances live and in the studio.
